# Success Metrics

This document defines the measurable outcomes the product is accountable for.
Each metric: what is measured, its baseline (from `current-state.md`), the target,
and how it is measured. Metrics are technology-agnostic and user-observable —
"p95 task completion under 30 seconds", never "uses caching". Distinguish
buildable verification targets (which flow into requirements and tests) from
post-launch business KPIs (which do not become acceptance criteria).

## Metric template

### <Metric name>

- **Measures**: _the outcome, in persona terms_
- **Baseline**: _current value and source_
- **Target**: _value and date_
- **Measurement**: _instrument and cadence_
- **Kind**: _verification target | business KPI_
