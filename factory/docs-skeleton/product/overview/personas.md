# Personas

This document names the roles that appear as `<role>` in every user story
(`../features/README.md`). Each persona gets: who they are, what they are trying
to accomplish, what blocks them today, and the words they use (which feed
`docs/domain/glossary.md`). Requirements may not invent roles that are not
defined here.

## Persona template

For each persona:

### <Persona name>

- **Who**: _role and context_
- **Goals**: _what success looks like for them_
- **Pain points**: _what blocks them today (trace to `current-state.md`)_
- **Vocabulary**: _terms they use; canonicalize in the glossary_
